About Quasar Contact

Secure, private, and end-to-end encrypted messaging

My Mission

Quasar Contact is dedicated to providing secure, private communication for everyone. In an age where digital privacy is increasingly under threat, I believe that secure communication should be accessible, reliable, and completely private.

What I Do

I've built a messaging platform that implements military-grade end-to-end encryption, ensuring that your conversations remain completely private. My zero-knowledge architecture means that I cannot access your messages, even if I wanted to.

Technology Stack

Quasar Contact is built using modern web technologies including Angular 18, Node.js, Socket.IO, and MongoDB. The encryption is handled client-side using the Web Crypto API, ensuring maximum security and privacy.

Privacy First

Privacy is at the core of everything I do. I collect minimal data (only email addresses for user identification), implement end-to-end encryption, and maintain a zero-knowledge architecture. I do not sell, share, or analyze your personal data.
