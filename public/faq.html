Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Quasar Contact

What is Quasar Contact?

Quasar Contact is a secure, end-to-end encrypted messaging platform that prioritizes your privacy. I use military-grade encryption to ensure your conversations remain completely private and secure.

How does the encryption work?

I use client-side encryption with the Web Crypto API, implementing AES-GCM encryption. Your messages are encrypted on your device before being sent, and only the intended recipient can decrypt them. The server never has access to your encryption keys or plaintext messages.

What data do you collect?

I collect minimal data - only your email address for user identification and authentication. I do not collect, store, or analyze your message content, contacts, or any other personal information. All messages are stored encrypted and cannot be read by me.

Is Quasar Contact free to use?

Yes, Quasar Contact is currently free to use during the alpha stage. I'm focused on building the best secure messaging platform and gathering user feedback.

Can you read my messages?

No, I cannot read your messages. My zero-knowledge architecture ensures that all encryption and decryption happens on your device. Even if I wanted to, I don't have the keys to decrypt your messages.

Is the source code available?

I believe in transparency and plan to open-source parts of my codebase in the future. This will allow security researchers and the community to audit my encryption implementation.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time through the settings page. When you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently removed from my servers, including your encrypted messages.

Is the source code available?

We believe in transparency and plan to open-source parts of our codebase in the future. This will allow security researchers and the community to audit our encryption implementation.

What happens if I lose my device?

Your encryption keys are stored locally on your device. If you lose your device, you'll need to log in again on a new device, which will generate new keys. Messages sent before the key change cannot be decrypted on the new device for security reasons.

Do you comply with GDPR?

Yes, I am fully GDPR compliant. I collect minimal data, implement privacy by design, and give you full control over your data. You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time.

How can I report a security issue?

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact me immediately at artem.turlenko@quasar.contact. I take security issues very seriously and will respond promptly to any reports.

Will there be mobile apps?

While Quasar Contact already works great on mobile browsers and can be installed as a PWA, I'm considering native mobile apps in the future based on user demand and feedback.
